## BOM
- 2 LED (Blue + White)
- 2 NPN Transistor
- 1 Photoresistor
- 1 47k Resistor
- 2 220 Resistor
- 1 10k Resistor
- 1 1k Resistor
- 1 Battery Holder
- 1 Push Button
- 1 Vibrating Motor
- 1 Diode
